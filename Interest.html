My Interests Hobbies

Swimming

My affinity for water began at the age of eight, when my first dive marked the beginning of a journey through the world of swimming. Every week for years, the pool became my training ground, where dedication and discipline shaped my strokes. Joining a competitive club fueled my passion, as I reveled in the camaraderie and the joy of pushing my limits in competitive swimming. Even during my university years, I returned to the familiar embrace of the pool, appreciating how it not only strengthened my body, but washed away stress and ignited a deep sense of joy. Swimming transcends the realm of mere sport; It is a lifelong pursuit that embodies both my physical and mental well-being.

Watching TV series and Movies

As the daily grind fades away and free time arrives, I find myself engrossed in the various narratives spinning on the screen. English TV series transport me to foreign countries and complex storylines, while Korean and Thai dramas offer fresh cultural perspectives and engaging plots. But my cinematic adventures are not bound by language; I washed the films in their original language and enjoyed the nuanced performances and authentic rhythms of the dialogues. And don't forget the cartoons! Whether presented in the vibrant colors of English animation or the playful charm of Sinhala dubs, these animated stories hold a special place in my heart, offering lighthearted escape and timeless lessons. Through this kaleidoscope of audio-visual storytelling, my imagination has been ignited, my cultural understanding expanded, and my spirit invigorated.

Dancing

Dance has been like magic to me since childhood. It started from fourth grade when I learned our amazing Sri Lankan rhythms and since then I have loved going to music. Every twist and turn makes me happy like a secret smile inside. I danced on stage in school plays and even university functions. It's a way to connect with others, feel creative and free without saying a word. Dance is not just a hobby, it's my happy place!
