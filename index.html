About

Aspiring IT student dedicated to knowledge, curiosity, and growth.

I am a relentless pursuit of excellence fueled by an unwavering passion for technology and innovation. As an undergraduate student at Rajarata University of Sri Lanka, I embody the spirit of continuous learning, striving to transform challenges into opportunities for growth.

My journey has been marked by a commitment to excellence in IT, reinforced by a Diploma in 3D Animation and a Certificate in Business English. Beyond academia, I thrive in the dynamic intersection of creativity and functionality, excelling in mobile front-end development, web development, and graphic design. My projects, such as a Unity 3D-based mobile app for landscape solutions and a web app spotlighting travel destinations, reflect not only technical expertise but also a deep passion for crafting meaningful user experiences.

I am not just a student; I am an active contributor working as Head of Graphic Design for Women in FOSS and a dynamic member of Rajarata AIESEC. Driven by a vision to make a positive impact in the world of technology, I embrace challenges as stepping stones to innovation, with the constant belief that the best is yet to come in this exciting journey of exploration and growth.
