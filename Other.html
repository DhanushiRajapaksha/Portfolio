Women in FOSS

Since 2023, I've proudly led as the Graphic Design Lead for Women in FOSS, shaping the organization's visual identity and contributing to impactful design projects. It's more than graphic design; it's about championing gender diversity in the open-source community. This role has allowed me to merge my passion for design with a commitment to fostering inclusivity in the world of Free and Open Source Software.
