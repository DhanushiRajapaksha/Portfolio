About Hi, I'm Dhanushi

A journey of curiosity, versatility and continuous growth

From studying IT to leading Graphic Design for Women in FOSS and contributing to AIESEC, I embody a holistic approach. A master of technology, languages and creativity, I aspire to inspire through my story of resilience and exploration.

About Me

In retrospect, my educational journey was a deliberate and varied exploration marked by key experiences that significantly contributed to my personal and professional development.

From 2005 to 2018, the foundation stage of Yasodara Devi Girls College, Gampaha instilled in me not only academic discipline but also a passion for learning. This period laid the foundation for my later ventures.

It became a conscious decision to diversify my skill set during my Certified Diploma in 3D Animation at Esoft Metro Campus in 2019. This venture into the creative field not only broadened my technical expertise, but also instilled a passion for infusing creativity into technology.

A major milestone in improving my communication skills was achieved in 2019 with the Certificate in Business English from the University of Colombo. This deliberate effort reflects my understanding of the important role effective communication plays in any career journey.

My pursuit of the National Vocational Qualification L-3 at the Institute of Technology and Computer Training in 2016 was an early introduction to practical training, laying the foundation for my technical knowledge and setting the stage for a commitment to continuous learning.

Integrating soft skills such as critical thinking, teamwork, creativity, and time management with proficiency in tools such as Android Studio, VS Code, Photoshop, and Unity 3D has been essential to my professional development.

Active participation in organizations has been a cornerstone of my personal and professional growth. Serving as the Graphic Design Lead for Women in FOSS and contributing to Rajarata AIESEC has not only honed specific skills but also instilled in me a sense of leadership and community involvement.

Being involved in various projects has been transformative. The 3rd year group project, which involved a Unity 3D-based mobile application for landscaping solutions, taught me collaboration and real-world problem solving. An individual project—a web application for travel destinations—demonstrated my initiative in creating user-centered solutions. My Flutter and Dart-based mobile app for recipes underscores my adaptability to emerging technologies.

Beyond formal endeavours, my various interests and hobbies, from swimming to web development, graphic design and enjoying cartoons, films and books, contribute significantly to my holistic development.

On reflection, each experience, activity and project has played a unique role in my professional development. My journey reflects a deliberate effort to foster a well-rounded skill set with an emphasis on diversity, adaptability, creativity, leadership and effective communication. I believe these qualities are the foundation for a successful and fulfilling career, regardless of the specific industry.
